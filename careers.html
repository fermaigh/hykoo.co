Back-end Engineer

Hykoo is looking to hire an experienced back-end engineer to help launch our ground-breaking mobile platform.


About you:

  • You have worked on high-availability RESTful APIs that have served massive consumer-scale audiences.
  • You know ruby and rails inside and out, and enjoy leveraging their cool quirks.
  • You’ve spent some time making the most of NOSQL database servers, particularly Mongodb, and you also have some good ideas about how to work with their limitations.
  • You are keenly aware of how code can get unwieldy, and follow some practices for how to avoid that.
  • The APIs you’ve worked on have served mobile clients.
  • You rock the server side, but you also keep the entire architecture in mind, and can come up with new ways for the client and server to do their dance as smoothly as possible.
  • You can make the rest of our team think of the world in ways that we haven’t considered before.
  • You delve into some HTML, CSS and Javascript, when necessary, without breaking a sweat.

Extra credit:

  • AWS EC2 configuration experience.
  • iOS and other C programming environments.
  • BS in Computer Science or related field (or equivalent).
